About Us

Learn more about WillBry Agro-Innovations Limited, our mission, vision, and the values that drive us.

Who We Are

Our Mission

To harness innovation, artificial intelligence, and modern processing technologies to transform agricultural produce into high-value products while empowering farmers and promoting food security.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading agri-innovation enterprise driving sustainable food systems through technology, value addition, and youth-centered agricultural transformation.

Core Values

Innovation – Continuous adoption of new ideas and smart technologies.
Integrity – Transparency and ethical business practices.
Sustainability – Environmentally responsible production systems.
Excellence – Commitment to superior quality.
Empowerment – Supporting farmers, youth, and rural communities.

Background & Rationale

Agriculture remains the backbone of Uganda's economy, yet farmers continue to face challenges related to post-harvest losses, limited access to processing technologies, and fluctuating market systems. WillBry Agro-Innovations Limited was founded to address these challenges through value addition, technology adoption, and digital transformation in the agricultural sector. Through leveraging modern processing equipment and digital systems, the company aims to transform primary produce especially potatoes into high-quality products such as crisps and fortified potato flour, while connecting farmers to real-time market intelligence.
